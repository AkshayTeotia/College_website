Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.PHARMA)


ABOUT

Pharmaceutical Research is prominent area today in terms of new discovery, fundamental research, new business opportunities, and significant contribution to health care areas for a healthy and prosperous tomorrow. To fulfill the need of the pharmaceutical industry for disciplined and competitive employees, Sanskar College of Pharmacy and Research is imparting technical education in Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) course since 2005. The Institute is approved by Pharmacy Council of India(PCI), New Delhi and is one of the fastest growing institutes in NCR region, providing value-based quality education and high level of motivation to the students so that they will have outstanding ability to contribute significantly to the development of health care in the country for a healthy and prosperous tomorrow.

Bachelor of Pharmacy is a four-year undergraduate program that has been designed to fulfill the need of the pharmaceutical industry for learned pharma professionals. During the program, students study many subjects including core pharmacy subjects like Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Analysis, and Pharmacognosy. The program helps students in developing the skills essential to counsel patients about right medication and use of drugs.



Admission Eligibility Criteria :
Passed intermediate 10+2 examination with English, Physics and Chemistry as one of the compulsory subjects with Mathematics or Biology as optional subjects.

AND

Obtained at least 45% marks (40% marks in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in the above subjects taken together.
