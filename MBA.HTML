Master of Business Administration (MBA)


ABOUT

MBA provides an understanding and skills related to the use of Management and its applications in both theoretical and practical concepts. The basic objective of the programme is to provide students an in-depth knowledge of Management and Business Concepts and to prepare students for the better carrier opportunities & further studies.

Skill-sets required for MBA

Students after completing an MBA course must have some of the skills after completing postgraduation. Python, SQL, and PHP are some of the skills useful for an MBA student. Some of the other skills required for MBA students are mentioned below:

    Data Analysis: R, SPSS, Tableau
    Programming Languages: Python, SQL, PHP
    Presentation and Design: PowerPoint, Canva, Prezi, Haiku Deck
    Technical Writing: SEO.
