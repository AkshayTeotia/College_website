Bachelor of Architecture (B.ARCH)


ABOUT

Architecture is the art and science of designing buildings and other physical structures in their sociological, technical and environmental contexts. This five-year full-time curriculum combines theoretical and practical knowledge, with critical input from specialists in the disciplines of arts, humanities, engineering, and technology, as well as professional practice of architecture. A strong foundation of multi-disciplinary enabling future architects and designers to become active contributors and leaders in making the world a better place.

Sanskar College of Architecture & Planning continues to nurture young aspiring minds to conceptualize the architectural wonder and inspires its students to graduate as architect of substance by imparting quality education through innovative teaching methodologies. The faculty is committed to mentor and motivate the students to develop a passion for lifelong learning in order to face the challenges in an increasingly competitive and creative sector.

Skill-sets required for B.ARCH
Passed 10+2 examination with following skills:
  • Good communication skills
  • Immense physical stamina
  • Excellent memory
  • Patience
  • Team spirit
  • Responsibility
  • Accountability
  • Creative Thinking Skills
  • Observant Attitude


Admission Eligibility Criteria :


For admission in 5 Year B. Arch program, it is must for Student to qualify NATA Exam, organized by Council of Architecture

AND

Secured 50% marks in 10+2 or equivalent examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM) as compulsory subjects

OR

Secured 50% marks in 10+3 Diploma with Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
