ABOUT US
Sanskar Educational Group (SEG), Ghaziabad was established in 2005 and is affiliated and approved by AICTE, PCI, CoA and affiliated to AKTU.
It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, research level and diploma programs in the disciplines of Engineering & Technology, Management, Pharmacy and Architecture.
Admission to SEG Admission is done on the basis of various entrance exams. In addition to this, the institute also provides 100% placement assistance to the students.
More than 200 students got placed through pool campus drives and more than 75 companies visited for on-campus recruitment.
Some of the top recruiters are Tech Mahindra, Aryson Technologies, Bajaj Capital Ltd., Justdial, HCL Technologies, etc.


Vision:

To groom young minds, hone their technical and managerial skills, liberate their talent and commitment and seek quality, innovation and creativity in them so as to prepare them to face challenges of life in the professions they choose. SGIT is committed to groom professionals par excellence. The institute has all the necessary infrastructure and facilities for imparting quality education in the field of technology as well as that of management. Apart from excellence of academic delivery, we at SGIT do our best to inculcate the qualities facilitating the entry into the corporate world. With student centric academic & operational philosophy, we ensure that our students grow academically, professionally, socially as well as spiritually in a stimulating campus environment.

Mission:

Our mission is to prepare the most competent and ingenious technocrats. Our foray into the world of Engineering, Technology, pharmacy and management is a means for facilitating higher education, training and research activities that affect the industrial development and economic growth of our nation. Simultaneously, it is our intent to contribute towards the goals of setting new trends internationally. We believe in power of education and we believe in imparting education thoroughly and impartially. We strive to grow from strength to strength as we traverse new horizons every day, by fusing intellect into wisdom ,sharpening perspective and weaving values into our dynamism.
