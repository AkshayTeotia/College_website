POLYTECHNIC


ABOUT

Polytechnic is a diploma or vocational course in which an institute focuses on delivering technical education. Generally, polytechnic courses are a three year programme after completion of which candidates obtain a certificate. Polytechnic also helps students to start their career by getting a good job at a reputed company.

The department of polytechnic started as roots of engineering in SGIT from the academic year 2013-14, affiliated to Board of Technical Education UP, Lucknow and is approved by AICTE. A three year diploma course has been offered in Mechanical engineering (Production) and Civil engineering. Our concentration is to impart best practical education by furnishing our workshop and labs with updated technology equipments.

POLYTECHNIC VS B.TECH

Polytechnic and B.Tech courses may both be successful in helping you prepare for a career. However, a polytechnic course provides you with a diploma, whereas a B.Tech course provides you with a degree. B.Tech courses generally have more expensive fee structures than polytechnic courses, and they often require four years to complete compared to the three years that polytechnic courses require. However, it is possible to earn both of these qualifications, and earning a polytechnic diploma may help you earn a B.Tech degree more easily.



Admission Eligibility Criteria :


For admission in Polytechnic you are required to complete 10th standard to obtain a secondary school leaving certificate (SSLC). While in school, it is important to focus on succeeding in science, maths and English courses.

It may be possible to pursue a polytechnic course after completing 12th. Colleges may determine your admission into diploma programmes based on your marks in 12th
